🐾 What is a Corgi?

Corgis (Pembroke Welsh Corgi), at a glance looks more like a fox. But to the advent Corgi lover they look like a cute herding dog. Corgis were bred for herding sheep,geese, and ducks. Corgis are also great at herding bigger animails like horses and even cattle. Queen Elizabeth II, brought the breed into the spotlight and were a sign of wealth. The Corgi is catergorize as a "Working Dog Breed",which surprises alot of people. I don't know why people get surprised when they see a dog with the body of a wiener dog and the head of a cattle dog.The vikings brought the breed with them when exploring Wales,to herd the wild animals of Wales.

🐾When did they come to light?

The Corgi breed has been around since 1107AD. They came into the eyes of the common people between the years 1925 and 1934,plus the Queen of Britian made them popular. Around 1925 The Kennel Club of Britian and The Corgi Club started to accept the Corgi breed into their clubs. The corgi was officaly recongnized by The Kennel Club as an offical breed in 1928.
